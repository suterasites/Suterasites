FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a website take?

Most sites launch in 1 to 3 weeks. Bigger sites with more pages or custom features can take longer. We'll give you a clear timeline before you commit.

Do I own the site?

One-off builds: yes, you get the full code. Subscription: the site stays on our hosting while you're subscribed. If you cancel after the 6-month minimum we can arrange export for a one-off fee.

What about hosting and domain?

Subscription covers both. One-off builds: we'll set it up on Cloudflare Pages (free tier) or your provider of choice.

Can I update the site myself?

Usually we handle updates because it's faster than walking you through the code. If you need a CMS, we can scope one (Decap, Sanity or similar) as part of the build.

What if I already have a site?

We migrate content, preserve your existing URLs for SEO, redirect old pages where needed. You won't lose rankings if we do it right.
