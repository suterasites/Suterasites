FAQ

Common questions.

Do you handle the printing too?

We supply print-ready PDFs to your printer of choice, or coordinate printing through our preferred local printers if you'd rather not deal with that step.

How long does design work take?

Business cards in 2-3 days. Capability statements in 1-2 weeks depending on content. Logos in 1 week for initial concepts.

Do I get the source files?

Yes. You get the original vector files (AI, PDF) plus exports in all common formats. It's your brand, we don't hold it hostage.

How many revisions are included?

Typically 2 rounds of revisions on the final design. We do enough discovery up front that third-round overhauls are rare.

Need collateral that actually represents you?

Every design job is quoted individually. Tell us what you need and we'll tell you the price.
